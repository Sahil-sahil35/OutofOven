From Our Oven, to Your Heart

Out of Oven began with a simple belief: baking is love made edible. Founded in 2018, we've been crafting exceptional baked goods that don't just taste good, but feel good. Every item tells a story of passion, tradition, and the pursuit of perfection.

We use time-honored recipes passed down through generations, combined with modern techniques and the purest ingredients. From our signature red velvet to our artisan breads, each creation is a testament to our commitment to excellence.
